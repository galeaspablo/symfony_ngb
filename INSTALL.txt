INSTALLATION NOTES

0) Copy every file from symfony_ngb (except README.md, .gitignore, INSTALL.txt) into your symfony installation folder
1) Append README.md to your README.md
2) APPEND .gitignore to your .gitignore
3) Read README.md!!!
4) Setup NodeJS, Gulp, and Bower using package.json, gulpfile.js, and bower.json
